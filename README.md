## Privacy Policy

iRaw IPTV Player does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app operates entirely on your device.

- **Credentials:** Your Xtream Codes / M3U credentials are stored locally on your device (Apple Keychain) and used only to connect to the playlist provider you choose. We do NOT send them to any third-party server.
- **Third-party metadata:** The app fetches movie posters and information from TMDB, Cinemeta, and Metahub using only public movie titles. No personal data is shared.
- **Analytics:** This app does NOT use any analytics or tracking.
- **Advertising:** This app contains NO advertisements.
- **Content:** iRaw IPTV Player does NOT host or provide any video content. Users supply their own playlist URLs from their personal subscriptions.

This privacy policy applies to the iRaw IPTV Player tvOS application.
